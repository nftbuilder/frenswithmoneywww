What is a NFT?
NFT stands for non-fungible token. A NFT is basically data that is accounted for in a digital ledger, and that data represents something specific and unique.Anyone thinking of buying an NFT should keep in mind that this is a new and speculative market with many unknown risks–including the risk of a 100 percent loss.NFTs have no inherent value like Art and are only worth what someone else would pay for it sometimes a million dollars sometimes nothing.

Ensure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the cost of a NFT plus the associated transaction (gas) fees. Approve the transaction on MetaMask and you are all set.
